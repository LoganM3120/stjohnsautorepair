About St. John's Auto Repair

Local, owner-operated, and dedicated to keeping you rolling

St. John's Auto Repair is the personal shop of ASE-certified master technician Lindon J. He delivers dealership-level expertise with neighborly care to drivers who visit his service bay in Jacksonville.

Meet Lindon

Lindon started St. John's Auto Repair after more than a decade servicing domestic and import vehicles in dealership bays. He saw how often customers felt stranded by unexpected breakdowns, long waitlists, and unclear pricing. His answer was to open a personal shop where every repair could be handled with care, clarity, and the right equipment.

  • ASE Master Certified technician
  • Privately operated service bay with OEM-grade diagnostics
  • Transparent digital estimates before any work begins
  • Respect for your schedule, budget, and time in the shop
